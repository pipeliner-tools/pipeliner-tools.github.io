Metal Loss Assessment

Evaluate remaining pipeline strength using ASME B31G, RSTRENG (Effective Area with River Bottom Profiling), Kastner, and NG-18 methodologies. Features batch Excel uploads and Safe Length vs. Depth acceptance curves.

Design Calculator

Compute required wall thickness and maximum internal design pressure per ASME B31.8 and API 5L. Automatically compares Location Class factors (1-4) and selects the next standard wall thickness.

NG Hydraulics

Perform natural gas flow calculations utilizing the AGA Fully Turbulent Flow equation and CNGA compressibility. Solve for network capacity, downstream pressure drops, or required pipe diameters while tracking erosional velocity limits.

Pig Tracking

Estimate theoretical transit times for gas or liquid runs, and dynamically project downstream SV station arrival times based on live, field-confirmed waypoint data.
